Property Description

This breathtaking Contemporary Glass House in Malibu represents the pinnacle of modern architectural design. Situated on a private cliffside overlooking the Pacific Ocean, this residence offers panoramic ocean views from every room through its floor-to-ceiling glass walls.

The 6,800 square foot masterpiece features an innovative open floor plan that seamlessly blends indoor and outdoor living. The main living area opens to an expansive deck with infinity-edge pool, outdoor fire pit, and multiple lounging areas perfect for entertaining or enjoying Malibu's spectacular sunsets.

The property includes five ensuite bedrooms, each with private ocean views. The master suite features a private balcony, spa-like bathroom with natural stone finishes, and custom walk-in closets. The gourmet kitchen boasts top-of-the-line appliances, custom cabinetry, and a waterfall island that doubles as a breakfast bar.
