Property Description

This stunning contemporary mansion redefines luxury living in the heart of Beverly Hills. With panoramic views of the city and mountains, this architectural masterpiece combines sophisticated design with state-of-the-art amenities.

Spanning 8,500 square feet across three levels, the residence features an open-concept living space with floor-to-ceiling windows, custom Italian marble flooring, and a chef's kitchen equipped with top-of-the-line appliances. The great room opens to an expansive outdoor entertainment area with infinity pool, outdoor kitchen, and lounge spaces.

The master suite is a private sanctuary with a spa-like bathroom, walk-in closets, and a private balcony. Five additional guest suites each offer ensuite bathrooms and custom finishes. Additional features include a home theater, wine cellar, fitness center, and smart home automation throughout.
