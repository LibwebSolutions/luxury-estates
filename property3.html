Property Description

Experience the pinnacle of urban luxury in this exquisite Downtown Luxury Penthouse, located in the heart of Manhattan. This 4,200 square foot residence occupies the entire top floor of one of New York's most prestigious buildings, offering breathtaking 360-degree views of the iconic city skyline.

The penthouse features an expansive open-concept living area with 12-foot ceilings, custom hardwood flooring, and floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the space with natural light. The gourmet chef's kitchen is equipped with premium stainless steel appliances, custom Italian cabinetry, and a massive marble island perfect for entertaining.

Four spacious bedrooms each feature ensuite bathrooms with premium fixtures and walk-in closets. The master suite includes a private terrace, spa-inspired bathroom with steam shower and soaking tub, and panoramic city views. Additional features include a private elevator entry, smart home automation, and access to the building's luxury amenities including a 24-hour concierge, fitness center, and rooftop lounge.
